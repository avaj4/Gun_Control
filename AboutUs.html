About Us

  • Our Mission

    • We are five diverse young women who came together at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at MetLife. We all wanted to learn more about computer science and ways to make a difference in the world.
    • As our generation grows up, the universal fear of mass shootings grows with us. These days, everywhere you go, the fear of being shot follows you. For instance, simple acts such as going to school, spending time with friends and family, or living your everyday life pose a threat to your life.
    • Because of this, it was decided that our final project should raise awareness to gun violence and recent mass shootings. We hope you've become more aware of this growing issue and that you have it in your heart to donate.
